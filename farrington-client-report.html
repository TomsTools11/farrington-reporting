04

Recommendations

1
Concentrate budget on the lowest-cost winnable sources
We raised the bids on Email and Premium Paid Search 2 — the cheapest inventory the campaign can win — to place both above their first-position market price.
2
Normalize four premium-search sources
Four sources carried elevated bids the market does not clear. We returned them to standard to remove overpay and redirect that spend to the winnable sources.
3
Hold Premium Referral
The source delivering 76% of leads at position 2.2 is held at its current bid.
4
Import current dispositions and enable policy tracking
Connecting contact, quote, and bind outcomes lets reporting show contact rate, quote rate, and cost per policy, and directs budget toward the sources that close.
5
Pool-expansion lever, if more volume is wanted
The campaign currently excludes Los Angeles County and targets multi-car households only. Relaxing either expands the addressable pool.
